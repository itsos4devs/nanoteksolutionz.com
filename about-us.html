About Us

Our Mission

To empower humanity to lead a healthier life.

Here because we care.

Our journey started with an aspiration to create a healthcare company that would enable the human spirit to run free in an equalizing space, a place where people of all backgrounds, all ages, all genders and pronouns, rich and poor, could access evidence-based care and lead a healthier life together.

Because we believe we each possess within ourselves at every moment of our lives, under all circumstances, the power to transform the quality of our lives - we saw a need for a community-based approach to health that could deliver the most effective personalized care.

We know and respect that feeling of liberation from pain symptoms that allow you to move freely and achieve your wildest imaginations, and that feeling is what we are championing here for you at Voya Health.

Our social responsibility, as we design the next frontier of healthcare, is to create an environment where one's inward journey towards recovery has a means for outward connection with others. We are all in this together and, it’s true, we are truly better together.
